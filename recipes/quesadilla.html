Quesadillas

Description

Are you looking for an appetizer before you eat street tacos? A quesadilla is quick and easy to make with very minimal ingredients. You can even enjoy this cheesy goodness with pickled carrots as a side.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Your favorite cheese (or cheeses)

Steps

  1. Very lightly oil a pan and place on medium heat.
  2. Before the pan gets too hot, place a tortilla on the pan.
  3. Cover half of the tortilla with your favorite cheeses.
  4. To avoid burning the tortilla, rotate the tortilla every 30 seconds or sooner.
  5. Once you start to see the cheese melt, fold the uncovered tortilla side over the cheese side and press down.
  6. After a minute, the cheese will fuse to the top of the tortilla. Carefully flip the quesadilla over and cook for another minute.
  7. Remove the quesadilla and allow to cool for a minute.
  8. You can slice the tortilla into triangles for easy servings.
  9. Enjoy with your favorite salsa or sour cream!
