Street Taco's

Description

Have you ever wanted to eat a delicious meal without breaking the bank? No look further! Here we will make carne asada street tacos that will satisfy your hunger and make you come back for more!

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas - Small Size
  • Carne Asada Meat
  • Diced White Onion
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Red or Green Salsa
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Start by dicing your whole white onion into small pieces.
  2. Next, finely chop up your cilantro and discard the ends.
  3. Chop the stem off the jalapenos and slice into quarters.
  4. Lightly oil your favorite pan and place the pan on the stove on medium heat.
  5. Once the pan is hot, place your carne asada meat on to the pan.
  6. Lightly oil a small pan and place on the stove at medium/low heat.
  7. Be sure to stir and cook the meat thoroughly. Add quartered jalapenos to the meat pan.
  8. While the meat is cooking, put the corn tortillas on the pan and flip every minute.
  9. As tortilla's finish cooking, remove and put on a plate. Do this for the amount of tacos you want.
  10. Once the meat is thoroughly cooked. Place the meat on a cutting board and chop it up. Place chopped up pieces into a bowl and cover.
  11. Place line of meat on the center of the tortilla.
  12. Add chopped onions and cilantro on top of the meat.
  13. Pour red or green salsa over the vegetables and meat.
  14. Sprinkle cotija cheese on top of the salsa.
  15. Fold the tortilla sides up, and enjoy with pickled carrots!
